Our Story

Highschool

Justin Thai and Dajia Thomas begin their relationship after prom! March 23, 2013

Their love story began when they met in 9th grade. Little did they know what their friendship would turn into. Fast forward to senior year, Justin asked Dajia to prom.

College

Dajia moved away to New York to attend LIM College and Justin stayed in Gwinnett to attend GGC and later transferred to Georgia State. The physical distance between them was great but their emotional connection was greater.

7 years of beautiful memories later, in August 2020...Dajia said "Yes!"

Their Aspirations

As many may already know, Justin is a dreamer and Dajia is a planner. Together they are limitless. As time progresses they expect to start a family of their own and travel the world. They believe in the gospel and hope to share it with as many people they can reach.
